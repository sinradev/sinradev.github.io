An Innovative Solution Born from Experience and Challenges in Software Development

Sinra is the result of a deep reflection on the limitations of traditional project management methodologies, whether Agile or Waterfall. Two major observations guided its creation

Agile pitfalls

The time-consuming nature of meetings and adjustments in Agile:

In an Agile environment, meetings pile up: sprint planning, reprioritization, endless discussions about which features to develop or not, and prioritizing bugs.

While these exchanges are essential, they often lead to a significant loss of time, reducing the team's overall efficiency.

Waterfall pitfalls

The loss of control in Waterfall development:

Conversely, the Waterfall methodology faces another challenge: deadlines. Projects frequently exceed their allotted time, and late adjustments are difficult to integrate into a rigid schedule.

This leads to a loss of control over timelines and deliverables, often at the expense of quality or stakeholder satisfaction.
