# The Sinra Project: An Innovative Solution Born from Experience and Challenges in Software Development

Sinra is the result of a deep reflection on the limitations of traditional project management methodologies, whether Agile or Waterfall. Two major observations guided its creation:

| <img src="/assets/images/agility.jpg" alt="Agility" style="float: left; margin-right: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px"> | 1. **The time-consuming nature of meetings and adjustments in Agile:** In an Agile environment, meetings pile up: sprint planning, reprioritization, endless discussions about which features to develop or not, and prioritizing bugs. While these exchanges are essential, they often lead to a significant loss of time, reducing the team's overall efficiency.| <img src="/assets/images/waterfall.webp" alt="Agility" style="float: right; margin-left: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px"> | 2. **The loss of control in Waterfall development:** Conversely, the Waterfall methodology faces another challenge: deadlines. Projects frequently exceed their allotted time, and late adjustments are difficult to integrate into a rigid schedule. This leads to a loss of control over timelines and deliverables, often at the expense of quality or stakeholder satisfaction. |

<br/>

## Sinra: The Perfect Balance Between Flexibility and Control

Designed to address the challenges of these two approaches, Sinra combines the best of Agile and Waterfall methodologies. This groundbreaking project provides a harmonious solution that:

- Reduces the burden of unnecessary meetings by promoting clear and structured planning.
- Maintains a global vision of deadlines and objectives while allowing flexibility to adapt to unforeseen events.
- Ensures enhanced control over developments with balanced and optimized cycles to prevent scope creep.

With Sinra, development teams regain sustainable efficiency, increased control, and a working method truly tailored to the realities of modern software development.
