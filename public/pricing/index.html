Pricing FAQ

What is Sinra?

Sinra is a modern project management tool that combines V-Model structure with Agile flexibility. It helps teams organize work around concrete releases, manage capacity planning, track issues and capabilities, and maintain unified documentation - all in one place.

How much does Sinra cost?

Sinra Pro costs €10 per user per month when billed monthly, or €96 per user per year (€8/month) when billed annually - saving you 20%. We offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

Can I try Sinra before purchasing?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start your trial. You can invite your team and test Sinra in a real project environment.

What's included in the Sinra Pro plan?

Sinra Pro includes unlimited projects and releases, advanced capacity planning, integrated QA management, rich text editor for specifications, multi-view intelligence (Kanban, List, Release), release management with checklists, custom workflows, and priority email and chat support.
